Jiggler Short Links Setup

Jiggler lets you share short links with other people in the company.

Setup for: Chrome Firefox

Chrome

Install the Jiggler extension.

Firefox

  1. Click on the plus in the search box and choose "Add Jiggler".
  2. Click on the plus in the search box and choose "Change Search Settings".
  3. Double-click to the right of Jiggler under the "Keyword" column. Put a "j" there.
  4. Now you can type (for example) "j link" in the address bar to go to that link.
